Reimagining the art of collaboration

Harness the power of your work while it’s in progress with tools that enhance the co-creative ability of 3D artists.

As an artist, much of your work is unseen. The drafts and mockups, inspiration boards and sketches –– these rarely make it out of the studio (or off your laptop) and into the hands of the people who engage with your final product. And more often than not, what gets lost in that translation is the best representative of the motivation behind a final image and the collaborative steps it took to get there.

At Artistry we aim to empower artists to represent the evolution of their work, without the hassle of photo journals and blog posts. We have crafted a set of tools specifically targeting 3D artists who are interested in collaborative works flows without sacrificing portability and flexibility. All of this is folded into our mobile app, providing you access to a collaborative 3D makespace on-the-go and a community of other artists to engage and share with.

A place for everything

Artistry provides a home for all those scraps, and sketches –– all the works in progress so you can find them in a moment when inspiration finally does strike.
